    About LinguaGo

    LinguaGo is a powerful and user-friendly language translation tool designed to make communication easier. With support for over 100 languages, instant translation, text-to-speech, and speech-to-text features, LinguaGo is your go-to solution for all your translation needs.

    How to Use LinguaGo

    1. Enter Text: Type or paste your text into the input box.
    2. Select Languages: Choose the source and target languages.
    3. Translate: Click the "Translate" button to get instant results.
    4. Listen: Use the text-to-speech feature to listen to the translated text.
    5. Save: Save or download your translations for future use.
