🔹 How to Use LinguaGo

  1. 📌 **Step 1:** Select **Input Language** and enter text.
  2. 🌍 **Step 2:** Choose **Target Language**.
  3. 🔁 **Step 3:** Click **Translate** and wait for the result.
  4. 🔊 **Step 4:** Click **Listen** to hear the translation.
  5. 📋 **Step 5:** Click **Copy** to save the translated text.
